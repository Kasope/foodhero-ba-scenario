# FoodHero: Business Analytics for Food Waste Reduction
AI-driven business analytics framework for reducing food waste through predictive customer behavior analysis

## Project Overview
A conceptual business analytics scenario exploring how data-driven insights can reduce food waste while creating business value. This academic project examines the integration of business analytics into a sustainable grocery platform, analyzing how data, technology, and business strategy combine to address real-world challenges.

**Type:** Proof of Concept

**Focus:** BA application in sustainable retail
